Why was Prism created?

PRISM act of 2007 is an electronic data collection that aims at protecting the USA according to the NSA.It was a top-secret and advanced surveillance program of the NSA.The PRISM program had a code-name US-984XN. According to the leak presentation,the NSA claimed that it launched the program to overcome the FISA warrant's pitfalls.The FISA warrant is in charge of tracking down and prosecuting foreign terrorists.But court mandates were needed before they can access and investigate the information of suspected criminals.The NSA wanted to overcome this challenge and have the flexibility of tracking downanyone when the body wants.Furthermore,the NSA noted that the USA has advantages in tracking down terrorists because most of the big tech companies reside in USA.

The PRISM program activities are shrouded in secrecy,which is not also helped by the constant denial of tech companies.But what the data suggest from leaked documents,the PRISM program relies on two aspects for its mass data collection. *Section 702 of Foreign Intelligence Survillance Act(FISA) *Section 215 of the US's Patriot Act. The Prism program law authorizes security agencies to retreive communications and metadata from telecommunication companies like AT&T,Verizon,Google,Yahoo,Facebook and other telecommunication and internet companies.
